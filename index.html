Projects

P-01 CFD Aerodynamics Jan 2024 – Apr 2025

CFD Analysis of NACA 0012 Airfoil with Slotted Flap

Conducted a full CFD study on the NACA 0012 airfoil across an angle-of-attack range of 0° to 20°, capturing coefficient of lift and drag alongside detailed flow visualisation. A slotted flap variant was modelled and compared against the baseline — quantifying the percentage increase in lift and drag, and examining differences in velocity contours and pressure distribution around both configurations.

Designed the slotted flap geometry in SolidWorks and executed the full simulation pipeline in Ansys Fluent, from mesh generation through post-processing of results.
